I'm a PhD candidate from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at New Jersey Institute of Technology, advised by Professor Qing Liu. My research targets High Performance Computing, with the focus on Lossy Compression, Scientific Data management and Machine Learning. I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering at New Jersey Institute of Technology in 2017 and B.S. degree in the Internet of Things engineering at Shandong University in 2015.
